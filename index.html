The Secret Santa

It’s very simple kind of a game, where you can make happy somebody or be happy by someone. The rules are very easy. You just need to register yourself by our form. Then you will get somebody’s locker number via student’s email, where you can leave your gift.
One more thing, leave opened your locker also, cause Santa will leave gift for you too :)

You have to include building to locker number. Otherwise, you may be miss your present
